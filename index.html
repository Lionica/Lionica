Maybe, it’s not goodbye

A song about love, loss and the hope for redemption. Take a moment to groove, smile and hopefully find a little joy in your life.

After years of writing together, playing in bands and experimenting with adulting, two childhood friends Jereimiah Doggart and Thomas Hamilton decided it was finally time to start something new. The goal was to fuse soulful melodies, blues rhythms and modern tones into a package that makes for pleasurable listening and drivetime singalongs. To make this dream a full reality, Isaias Zarazua was recruited as the third pillar in the group named Lionica.

Together, the music they create; which sounds like John Mayer and Coldplay got together for a jam, aims represent life in its entirety while bringing a smile to your face. Grab a drink, take a drive or pop in some ear buds for a nice walk and let Lionica be the soundtrack guiding you through your day.
